NOTIFICATION SOUND SETUP
========================

To add a notification sound to your app:

1. Download a notification sound file (MP3 format recommended)
   - Keep it short (1-2 seconds)
   - Similar to WhatsApp notification sound
   - You can find free notification sounds at:
     * https://notificationsounds.com/
     * https://www.zedge.net/
     * https://freesound.org/

2. Rename your sound file to: notification-sound.mp3

3. Replace the placeholder file at: public/notification-sound.mp3

4. Rebuild your project:
   npm run build
   
5. For APK: Sync Capacitor
   npx cap sync android

RECOMMENDED SOUND CHARACTERISTICS:
- Format: MP3
- Duration: 1-2 seconds
- Sample Rate: 44.1kHz
- Bit Rate: 128 kbps
- Volume: Normalized (not too loud or too soft)

The sound will play automatically when a new message arrives in any group.
